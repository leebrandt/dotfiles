If you're uncertain about an answer to something, leave the answer blank. But tell me _why_ you left it blank. Why are you unsure about it. It's likely a place for more questions. Something like:

"I didn't know about this, so I left it blank. I didn't know if you were doing {this thing} or {that}."

OR

"What do you think, here? I didn't answer because I didn't know {x}"

- Wrong answers are 3x worse than blank ones.
