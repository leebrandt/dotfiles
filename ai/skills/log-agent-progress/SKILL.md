---
name: log-agent-progress
description: Use this skill when you need to log the progress of an agent that you've dispatched to do work.
---

When you need to update the user on the progress of an agent you've dispatched to work a spec, say:

"The [agent-type] working on [spec-name] is currently: [current work status]. They are about [percentage complete] of the way done."

Examples:

- The dev-agent working on the 001-home-page-shell.md is currently test-driving the footer section of the page. They are about 80% done.
- The designer-agent working on 000-foundational-design.md is currently testing color palettes. They are about 20% done.

No need to be verbose, just an update that tells me _who_ it is, _what_ they are working on, _where_ they are in the process, and _how_ much longer.
